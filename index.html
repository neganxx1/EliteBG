THE BALLAS STORY

The Ballas have been active since the 1970s, controlling areas like Glen Park and Jefferson in Los Santos. They gained power through drug trafficking, arms dealing, and organized crime, forming alliances with corrupt law enforcement and cartels.

Over the years, the gang split into various sets, including Front Yard Ballas, Rollin' Heights Ballas, Kilo Tray Ballas, and Temple Drive Ballas, each controlling different territories.

The Ballas are known for their dominance in the drug trade and have always been in fierce rivalry with the Grove Street Families, fighting for control of the streets.

GANG RULES

  • Respect the gang hierarchy and leadership at all times
  • Never cooperate with law enforcement or rival gangs
  • Always defend gang territory and members
  • No unauthorized criminal activities that could bring attention to the gang
  • All members must contribute to gang operations and finances
  • Disputes between members are to be settled internally
  • Never reveal gang secrets or operations to outsiders
  • Members must represent the gang with pride and loyalty
  • Follow the instructions of superiors without question
  • Betrayal of the gang is punishable by expulsion or worse
